16.1.1 Determinants of wage data (CPS 1988)

Download the “Determinants of wage data (CPS1988)” dataset from here: https://vincentarelbundock.github.io/Rdatasets/csv/AER/CPS1988.csv

You can find documentation for this dataset here: https://vincentarelbundock.github.io/Rdatasets/doc/AER/CPS1988.html

  1. Write a for loop that produces a table of the group means of wage for each of the categorical variables ethnicity, smsa, region, and parttime. That is, for the parttime variable, the code would be (note here that you will be looping over variable names):
D |>
  group_by(parttime) |>
  summarize(mean_wage=mean(wage)) |>
  print()
## # A tibble: 2 × 2
##   parttime mean_wage
##   <chr>        <dbl>
## 1 no            640.
## 2 yes           234.
  1. For each increment of 10 years of experience, produce and save a histogram of weekly wages. Make sure that the plot heading shows which increment of experience you are plotting.
